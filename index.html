Privacy Policy for Days Matter

Last updated: May 25, 2026
Developer: JY Studio
Contact: support@jystudio.example.com
App package: com.jystudio.daysmatter

1. Introduction

Days Matter ("the App") helps you track anniversaries, countdowns, and important dates. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data the App processes, why, and what choices you have.

Summary: Your day entries are stored on your device by default. We do not sell your data. We do not show ads. We do not operate a user account system that uploads your content to JY Studio servers.

2. Data We Process

2.1 Data you create (stored locally)

  • Titles and notes for your days/events
  • Dates (Gregorian or lunar), countdown settings, reminders
  • Sort order, pinned status, archive and trash items
  • Theme and app preferences (e.g. haptics, layout mode)
  • Optional WebDAV server URL, username, and password (stored on device if you enable sync)

This data is saved in your device's local storage unless you choose to sync via WebDAV.

2.2 Data sent to third parties (only when you choose)

We do not upload your day content to JY Studio-owned servers.

2.3 Data we do NOT collect

  • Precise or coarse location
  • Contacts, SMS, call logs
  • Advertising ID for ad targeting
  • Analytics or crash reporting to third-party SDKs (not integrated in current version)

4. Home Screen Widgets

If you add a Days Matter widget to your home screen, the App renders a preview image stored locally on your device. Widget data is not sent to us.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Local data: Removed when you uninstall the App or clear app storage.
  • Trash: Items in Recycle Bin remain until you restore or permanently delete them.
  • WebDAV: Backups on your server are controlled by you.

6. Security

  • Local data stays on your device.
  • WebDAV sync uses the protocol and server you configure (HTTPS recommended).

7. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date will change. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact

Questions about privacy: support@jystudio.example.com
